## Frequently Asked Questions

## Is there a trial?

Yes, start with a free 14-day trial of Advanced Data Rooms and explore the platform’s features and benefits. After that, ‌you can sign up on your own. [Start your 14-day trial](https://docsend.com/signup/marketing?show_dropbox=1)

_\*Unlimited visitors not available on free trials._

## What is the difference between a user and a visitor?

A user is someone who has a DocSend account. Each user needs to have a DocSend license to access their DocSend account. A visitor is anyone that visits your content through the DocSend link you send. You can have unlimited visitors and unlimited visits to your content, visitors do not require a license to view your content through the DocSend link.

## What types of content can I send with DocSend?

DocSend supports just about any file type. Per-page analytics are available for PDF, Microsoft Word, Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Docs, Google Slides, and Keynote files. You can also send and track links to websites or download-only content such as video, spreadsheets, audio, text, and archive files.

## Can I use DocSend as a Virtual Data Room (VDR)?

Yes, DocSend [Advanced Data Rooms](https://www.docsend.com/features/virtual-data-room) are VDRs where sensitive business information is centralized to protect, organize, and share files needed to conduct high-stakes business deals, including mergers and acquisitions (M&As), due diligence processes, fundraising, and other investment activities. Access to the files in your data room is governed by a unique link that you control to determine the level of access each visitor or group has.

## What's a Space?

Spaces are what DocSend calls our data rooms. DocSend customers on team plans can create as many data rooms (Spaces) as they need. DocSend data rooms offers easy set-up, out-of-the-box integrations, [eSignatures](https://www.docsend.com/features/esignature), industry-backed templates, [real-time analytics](https://www.docsend.com/features/analytics), auto-indexing, custom branding, and granular data room permissions.

## My files are stored in the cloud. How do I get them into DocSend?

DocSend integrates with all major cloud storage providers: Box, Dropbox, Google Drive, and Microsoft OneDrive. Use our [Gmail extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/docsend-extension-for-chr/nmkphfcebcpicnakipcmgapcckkbhppd?hl=en) or our [Outlook extension](https://appsource.microsoft.com/en-us/product/office/WA104380091?tab=Overview) today and work faster and smarter right from your email.

## Do I have to pay for each Data Room?

Unlike legacy virtual data room solutions, Dropbox DocSend provides unlimited data rooms and imposes no limits on the number of visitors you can invite. This flexibility lets you manage multiple deals simultaneously without worrying about space or access restrictions. DocSend gives your company a competitive edge over those still relying on methods like email with cloud storage.

## What makes DocSend Advanced Data Rooms secure for handling sensitive deals?

DocSend employs robust security measures, including data encryption, access controls, and audit trails, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of your sensitive information throughout the deal process. Visit our [Trust Center](https://trust.dropbox.com/?product=docsend&itemName=product_features&source=click) to learn more.
