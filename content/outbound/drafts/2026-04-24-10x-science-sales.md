## Personalization Notes
David Roberts is a co-founder of 10x Science (alongside Andrew Reiter and Vishnu Tejus). All three worked in Nobel laureate Carolyn Bertozzi's Stanford lab. The company raised a $4.8M seed in April 2026, led by Initialized Capital, backed by Y Combinator, Civilization Ventures, and Founder Factor. 10x Science is a SaaS platform that combines deterministic chemistry/biology algorithms with AI agents to characterize protein drug candidates for pharma companies. Founded December 2025. The company sells to pharma companies on a monthly SaaS basis.
