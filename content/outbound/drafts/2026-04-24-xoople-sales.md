## Personalization Notes
Fabrizio Pirondini is the co-founder and CEO of Xoople, a Spanish satellite constellation company developing Earth observation data for AI/deep learning applications. Raised a $130M Series B on April 6, 2026, led by Nazca Capital with participation from MCH Private Equity, CDTI, Buenavista Equity Partners, and Endeavor Catalyst. Partnership with L3Harris Technologies for sensor manufacturing. Total raised: $225M. Sells geospatial intelligence to government agencies, agribusiness, and large enterprise corporations. CEO described their mission as building "Earth's System of Record."
