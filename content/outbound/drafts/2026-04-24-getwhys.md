## Personalization Notes
Philippe Boutros is the co-founder and CEO of GetWhys (alongside Tyler Honsinger). GetWhys is a Boise, ID-based AI-powered customer intelligence platform that turns buyer research into go-to-market-ready intelligence (messaging, content, competitive materials). Customers include Intel, Verizon, Docusign, and Commvault. Raised a $5.2M "Seed II" round in 2026, led by Epic Ventures with participation from CEAS Investments and Portland Seed Fund. Previous backers Next Frontier Capital, Tuesday Capital, and Capital Eleven also participated. Total raised: ~$8M.
