## Personalization Notes
Fynn Glover is the CEO and co-founder of Schematic (alongside Gio Hobbins and Ben Papillon). Boulder, CO-based Schematic builds entitlements and enforcement infrastructure for SaaS and AI companies — decoupling pricing/packaging logic from code and enforcing it at runtime, with a Stripe App integration. Raised $6.5M seed in April 2026, led by S3 Ventures with participation from MHS, Active Capital, NextView, and Ritual. Angels include founders of LaunchDarkly, CrowdStrike, and Salesloft. Total raised: $12M since 2023 founding.
