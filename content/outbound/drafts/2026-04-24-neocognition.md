## Personalization Notes
Yu Su is an Ohio State University professor who founded NeoCognition to commercialize his research on self-learning AI agents. The company emerged from stealth on April 21, 2026 with a $40M seed round co-led by Cambium Capital and Walden Catalyst Ventures, with participation from Vista Equity Partners and notable angels including Intel CEO Lip-Bu Tan and Databricks co-founder Ion Stoica. NeoCognition builds AI agents that can autonomously learn and specialize in any domain, targeting enterprises and SaaS companies.
