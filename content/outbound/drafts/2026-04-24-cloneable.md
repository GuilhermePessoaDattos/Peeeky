## Personalization Notes
Lia Reich is a co-founder of Cloneable (alongside Tyler Collins and Patrick Lohman), which raised a $4.6M seed round on April 23, 2026, led by Congruent Ventures with participation from First In, Overline, Bull City Venture Partners, and St. Elmo Venture Capital. The Raleigh, NC-based startup builds AI agents that clone expert worker knowledge for heavy industries (energy, utilities, infrastructure, agriculture). Co-founders were founding employees at drone company PrecisionHawk. Total raised: $5.35M since 2023 founding.
