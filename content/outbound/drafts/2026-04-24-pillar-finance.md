## Personalization Notes
Harsha Ramesh is the CEO and co-founder of Pillar (alongside CTO Chinmay Deshpande). Pillar is an AI-powered financial risk management platform that automates hedging for commodity-driven businesses in metals, food, and aviation. Raised a $20M seed round led by Andreessen Horowitz on April 14, 2026, with participation from Crucible Capital, Gallery Ventures, and Uber CEO Dara Khosrowshahi. Total raised: $23M. Founded 2023, based in San Francisco.
