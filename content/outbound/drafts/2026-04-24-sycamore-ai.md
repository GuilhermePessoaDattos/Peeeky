## Personalization Notes
Sri Viswanath is the founder and CEO of Sycamore, former CTO of Atlassian and former Coatue partner. Sycamore raised a $65M seed round on March 30, 2026, co-led by Coatue and Lightspeed. The company builds a trusted operating system for enterprise AI agents — handling security, governance, and human oversight across agent deployments. Notable angels include Bob McGrew (former OpenAI chief scientist), Lip-Bu Tan, Ali Ghodsi, Frederic Kerrest (Okta co-founder), and Mike Knoop (Zapier co-founder). Founded fall 2025.
