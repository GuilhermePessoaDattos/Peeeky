## Personalization Notes
Mike Henry is the CEO of Parasail, which came out of stealth and raised a $32M Series A in April 2026, led by Touring Capital (Samir Kumar) and Kindred Ventures (Steve Jang). Parasail orchestrates distributed GPU compute across 40 data centers in 15 countries for AI model inference, generating 500 billion tokens daily. The company targets early-stage startups and AI developers who need GPU access without long-term commitments. Parasail competes with Fireworks AI and Baseten.
