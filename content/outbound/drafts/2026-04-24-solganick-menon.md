## Personalization Notes
Ramesh Menon joined Solganick as Managing Director on March 26, 2026, covering Technology Services and Digital Transformation M&A. Menon is based in Plano, TX, and brings nearly 30 years of experience, including roles as Chief Business Officer of L&T Technology Services, CEO of a services startup, and senior leadership at HCL Technologies and CSC. Solganick is an independent investment banking and M&A advisory firm focused on software and IT services, founded in 2009. They specialize in sell-side and buy-side transactions for PE-backed and founder-led companies across cybersecurity, MSPs, digital transformation, AI/data analytics, and cloud consulting.
