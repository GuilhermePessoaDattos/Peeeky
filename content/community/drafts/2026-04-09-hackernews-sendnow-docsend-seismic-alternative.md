## Context

A builder frustrated with Highspot/Seismic pricing built a free file tracking tool and posted it on HN (January 2026). The thread explicitly covers the core Peeeky use case: upload files, share link, see who opens, page-level analytics. Low engagement (2 upvotes, 1 comment) means the comment section is still open. The creator mentioned "AI features for optimal outreach timing" as a future plan — Peeeky already does this with recipient AI chat, so a technical response can advance the conversation without being dismissive.

## Proposed Response

The Highspot/Seismic pricing model is genuinely frustrating — charging by employee headcount for a tool where value scales with deals closed, not company size, was always strange.

On the AI outreach timing angle you mentioned: the most actionable version of this we've found isn't just "they opened at 2pm on Tuesday" but "they re-opened the document 48 hours later and spent 4 minutes on the pricing section." That second event is a much cleaner trigger for a rep to pick up the phone than any first-open notification. The recency + section-specificity combo filters out the "opened on mobile while commuting and immediately closed" noise.

The harder product question in this space is whether you let recipients interact with the document directly — AI chat so they can ask the proposal questions without having to email the rep. The engagement data you get from that (what they asked, what they were confused about) is often more useful than page-time alone. Curious if that's in scope for your roadmap.
