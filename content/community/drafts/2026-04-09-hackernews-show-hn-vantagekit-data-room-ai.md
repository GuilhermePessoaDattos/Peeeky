## Proposed Response

Great to see more builders tackling this. The gap between "just share a Dropbox link" and a full enterprise VDR has been genuinely underserved.

One thing we've found really moves the needle for founders sharing pitch decks specifically: page-level time-on-slide data combined with recipient AI chat. When an investor spends 90 seconds on your "Market Size" slide and then asks the AI three follow-up questions about TAM methodology, that's a much richer signal than "viewed for 4 minutes." The follow-up conversation you then have is completely different — you go in knowing exactly where the skepticism lives.

The staging/live-sync with Google Docs idea is clever; the PDF export loop is genuinely annoying. Curious how you handle version tracking when a document gets updated after a recipient has already engaged with an earlier version — does their prior session data get attributed to the old version or merged?
