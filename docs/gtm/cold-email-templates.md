## Usage Notes

- **Send volume:** Max 30-40 personalized emails per day to maintain deliverability.
- **Follow-up cadence:** Day 3, Day 7, Day 14. Stop after 3 touches if no response.
- **A/B testing:** Split subject lines 50/50 on each segment. Track open rates weekly and promote winners.
- **Personalization is mandatory:** Never send these as-is. Spend 2-3 minutes per email adding specific details about the recipient.
- **Reply handling:** Any reply (even "not interested") gets a human, thoughtful response within 4 hours.
