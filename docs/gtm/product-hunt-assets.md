## 3. Maker Comment

Hey Product Hunt -- Alex here, Head of Product at Peeeky.

I started building Peeeky after sending dozens of pitch decks into the void with no idea whether anyone actually read them. That uncertainty was painful, and I knew I was not the only one dealing with it.

Peeeky started as a simple document tracker: share a link, see who opens it. But as I talked to founders, sales teams, and consultants, the scope grew into something much bigger.

Today, Peeeky gives you:

- Per-page analytics so you know exactly which pages hold attention
- AI chat embedded in your shared documents so readers get instant answers
- Engagement scoring that tells you who is genuinely interested
- Data rooms with granular access controls and watermarking
- eSignature so you can close deals without switching tools
- Email verification to know exactly who is viewing your files

The free plan is genuinely useful, not a bait-and-switch. Pro is $39/mo and Business is $129/mo.

We built this because sharing documents should not mean losing visibility into what happens next. I would love your feedback, and I am here all day to answer questions.

## 4. First Comment (Response to "How is this different from DocSend?")

Great question. A few key differences:

First, Peeeky has AI chat built directly into the document viewer. Your readers can ask questions and get answers instantly without contacting you. DocSend does not offer this.

Second, our free plan is actually usable. You get tracking, analytics, and basic data rooms without paying anything.

Third, we include eSignature, engagement scoring, watermarks, and email verification in one platform. With DocSend, several of these require separate tools or higher-tier plans.

Fourth, pricing. Our Pro plan is $39/mo compared to DocSend's per-user pricing which adds up quickly for teams.

We respect what DocSend built. We just think document sharing deserves a more modern, AI-native approach.

## 7. Launch Day Checklist

### Two Weeks Before Launch

- [ ] Finalize all 5 screenshots (see section 6) -- ensure they are 1270x760px, clean, with realistic demo data
- [ ] Record a 1-2 minute product demo video showing the core flow: share document, track views, AI chat interaction
- [ ] Prepare the Product Hunt listing as a draft: tagline, description, maker comment, all media
- [ ] Reach out to 3-5 Product Hunt hunters with outreach message (see section 8)
- [ ] Build a list of 50+ supporters (friends, early users, fellow founders) who will upvote and comment on launch day
- [ ] Draft 10 personalized DMs to send to supporters the morning of launch
- [ ] Prepare responses for the 10 most likely questions (pricing, DocSend comparison, security, AI accuracy, free plan limits, data privacy, team plans, integrations, mobile support, API access)
- [ ] Write a launch day blog post for peeeky.com/blog explaining the Product Hunt launch and linking to the listing

### One Week Before Launch

- [ ] Confirm launch date (Tuesday through Thursday, ideally Tuesday)
- [ ] Schedule a Product Hunt "upcoming" page if available
- [ ] Test the sign-up flow end to end -- make sure peeeky.com handles a traffic spike
- [ ] Prepare a special offer for Product Hunt users (e.g., 3 months of Pro free, or extended free plan limits)
- [ ] Set up a dedicated landing page at peeeky.com/producthunt with the special offer
- [ ] Pre-write social media posts for launch day (LinkedIn, Twitter/X) -- do not post yet
- [ ] Brief the team: everyone should be online and responsive on launch day

### Launch Day (12:01 AM PT)

- [ ] **12:01 AM PT** -- Listing goes live. Post the maker comment immediately.
- [ ] **12:05 AM PT** -- Send the first round of DMs to your 10 closest supporters asking them to upvote and leave a genuine comment
- [ ] **6:00 AM PT** -- Send the second wave of notifications to your broader supporter list (email + DMs)
- [ ] **7:00 AM PT** -- Post on LinkedIn (use Post 1 from linkedin-posts.md, adapted to mention Product Hunt)
- [ ] **7:30 AM PT** -- Post on Twitter/X with a link to the Product Hunt listing
- [ ] **8:00 AM PT** -- Share in relevant Slack communities, Discord servers, and founder groups (no spamming, add genuine context)
- [ ] **Throughout the day** -- Respond to every comment on the Product Hunt listing within 30 minutes. Be genuine, helpful, and specific.
- [ ] **Throughout the day** -- Monitor the listing ranking. If dropping, send another wave of personalized outreach.
- [ ] **12:00 PM PT** -- Post a midday update on Twitter/X sharing early traction or an interesting comment from the listing
- [ ] **5:00 PM PT** -- Thank everyone who supported with a LinkedIn post or Twitter/X thread
- [ ] **End of day** -- Document final ranking, total upvotes, comments, and sign-ups for the retrospective

### Day After Launch

- [ ] Post a "here is what happened" retrospective on LinkedIn (use Post 10 from linkedin-posts.md as a template)
- [ ] Send a thank-you email to everyone who upvoted or commented
- [ ] Follow up with every person who signed up through Product Hunt within 48 hours

- [ ] Update the peeeky.com homepage with the Product Hunt badge if you earned one
